  Technical Manpower

ABE Oil & Gas Services supply technical Assistance, primed to mobilize at short notice and undertake a variety of roles in the oil and gas industry, can be essential to maintain smooth and seamless production.

The opportune intervention of experienced oil and gas Experts during the extraction process is crucial for maximising productivity, time, safety and cost-efficiency. With dependability and the suitability of technical manpower supply being key to your operational continuity, you need a partner you can rely on to respond to your needs and deliver a quality service. 

ABS technical manpower supply capabilities include the posting of consultants, skilled engineers and Experts, who can operate on both a permanent and ad-hoc basis. Prepared for service at short notice, they are available to undertake both onshore and offshore roles.

A select group of experts within our industry structure are available to undertake project work, holiday and sickness cover.

In order to match the skillset and suitability of our candidates with your requirements, we encourage you to participate in pre-job meetings with our personnel and manpower managers.
